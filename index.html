What is Korean face sculpting?
+
Korean face sculpting is a hands-on, manual therapy that works across the deeper layers of the face — including bone, fascia, and muscle — to naturally reshape, lift, and contour facial features from within. Unlike a conventional facial, which focuses on the skin's surface, Korean face sculpting addresses the structural root causes of sagging, facial asymmetry, and loss of definition. No needles, no surgery — just skilled, expert hands-on technique.
What is the Korean Golki bone reshaping method?
+
Golki is a traditional Korean face sculpting technique that works with the natural structure of the facial bones — applying gentle, precise manual pressure to guide the face toward a more symmetrical, V-shaped, and defined form. At Hanseol Beauty, Golki is performed by our trained Golki master as part of our Signature Hanseol Method — a programme uniquely curated by our founder, who is also a qualified chiropractor. This rare combination of Korean bone reshaping mastery and chiropractic anatomical expertise is what sets Hanseol apart: every movement is structurally informed, precisely calibrated, and deeply safe.
What is the difference between your face sculpting treatments?
+
The Korean Gua Sha & Lifting Facial focuses on releasing tension and restoring softness, making it ideal for those with tense jaw muscles or headaches. The V-Line Bamboo Detox Facial targets lymphatic drainage and definition. The Signature Korean Face Sculpting is our most comprehensive program — combining deep muscle stimulation, structural contouring, and lymphatic drainage for those seeking lasting transformation. Your therapist will recommend the most suitable starting point during your first visit.
Why do you pair face sculpting with body treatment?
+
Many facial imbalances are rooted in the body — tight neck muscles, uneven shoulder tension, or postural habits that pull the face out of alignment. By addressing the body alongside the face, our results are deeper and longer-lasting. Our Body Balancing treatment is designed specifically to support the face sculpting programme, so your investment in your face yields the full outcome it deserves.
Can I have Korean face sculpting if I've had botox or fillers?
+
Yes. Many of our clients complement their Hanseol treatments with other aesthetic procedures. Our face sculpting techniques work on the muscular and structural level, and can actually provide an excellent foundation canvas that enhances and supports the results of aesthetic treatments. We recommend informing us of any recent procedures so we can tailor the treatment appropriately. As a general guideline, we advise waiting at least 2 weeks after any injectable procedure.
How soon can I see results?
+
Many clients notice an immediate difference after their first session — a sense of lightness, a more lifted appearance, or reduced puffiness. More significant, lasting structural changes typically develop over a series of sessions. We recommend a commitment of at least 4–6 sessions to see meaningful transformation, though results vary based on individual skin condition, lifestyle, and the treatment chosen.
Is there any downtime or recovery after the treatment?
+
Most clients can see improvement from one session and leave looking and feeling refreshed with no downtime. Some people with more sensitive or thin skin may experience mild redness, warmth, or swelling in treated areas — this is normal and typically resolves within a few hours. You can return to your regular activities immediately after your session.

Is Korean face sculpting painful?
+
Face sculpting involves deep muscle stimulation similar to a deep tissue massage, so on areas of tension you may feel a deep pressure. Our therapists are trained to calibrate pressure to your comfort at all times. Communication during the treatment is always welcomed and encouraged.
Can facial asymmetry be corrected naturally without surgery?
+
Many cases of facial asymmetry are caused by muscle imbalance, jaw tension, postural strain, and structural misalignment — all of which can be gently and effectively addressed through Korean face sculpting. Our Signature Method works across bone, fascia, and muscle to improve facial balance and symmetry naturally, without surgery or injections. Results are gradual, cumulative, and deeply natural-looking.
How can I get a smaller, V-shaped face naturally in Malaysia?
+
A smaller, more V-shaped face can be achieved naturally through Korean face sculpting techniques that release jaw tension, stimulate lymphatic drainage to reduce facial puffiness, and manually contour the facial structure. At Hanseol Beauty in Kuala Lumpur, our V-Line Bamboo Detox Facial and Signature Korean Face Sculpting are both designed to slim, define, and achieve a more refined V-face — without botox or surgery.
How long do Korean face sculpting results last?
+
Results from Korean face sculpting are cumulative and build over time. Many clients see visible improvement from their very first session. With a consistent programme, results are typically maintained through monthly sessions. Lifestyle factors such as posture, stress levels, sleep, and hydration also influence how long results are retained between visits. Your therapist will design a personalised maintenance plan during your consultation.
Can face sculpting help me achieve a sharper jawline?
+
Yes. A sharper, more defined jawline is one of the most visible results of consistent Korean face sculpting. Through lymphatic drainage, deep muscle release, and precise manual contouring, we reduce jaw tension, decrease puffiness around the lower face, and restore natural jaw definition. Many clients notice a more chiselled, V-shaped jawline from their very first session at Hanseol Beauty, Kuala Lumpur.
